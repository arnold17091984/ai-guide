## FAQ for Implementation

### Q: What if we can't hit 1K users by week 8?
A: If you hit 500+ with strong engagement (40% D7 retention), that's acceptable. Scale before launch if needed.

### Q: Should we add monetization in MVP?
A: No. Get to 50K users first. Monetization comes in Phase 1.5.

### Q: How do we get seed content?
A: Founder writes 5-10 articles, recruit 5-10 expert contributors to write 10-20 more.

### Q: What if X API pricing spikes?
A: Have fallback sources (GitHub trending, Reddit, HN). Trending is differentiator but not foundation.

### Q: When should we hire team members?
A: Community manager by week 12, content editor by week 14, additional engineer if needed.

### Q: What's the biggest risk?
A: Low contributor activation rate. If < 3% of users publish, network effects won't work.

### Q: How do we compete with Stack Overflow?
A: We don't. SO is general; we're specialized. We verify skills; they don't. We surface trends; they don't.

### Q: Can we pivot if this doesn't work?
A: Yes. Pivot signals: retention < 25%, articles < 0.5/day, NPS < 30. Be ready to pivot by week 6.
