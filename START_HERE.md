## The Strategy in 3 Sentences

AI Guide becomes the community platform where developers share and discover verified Claude Code workflows, with real-time trending discussions from X (Twitter) as a differentiator.

We're addressing a clear market gap (no specialized platform for Claude Code + workflows + verification + trending) by building a community platform that creates network effects and defensible moat through specialization.

Launch MVP in 8 weeks with 11 focused features, validate product-market fit by hitting 1,000 users with 40%+ day-7 retention, then scale through skill verification, collaborative editing, and creator monetization.
